## What is a Digital Business Operator?

A **Digital Business Operator** is an AI agent that acts as an autonomous executive for your business. Unlike traditional automation tools that follow rigid rules, a Digital Business Operator:

1. **Understands Context** - Uses LLM to comprehend business situations
2. **Makes Decisions** - Evaluates options and takes appropriate actions
3. **Learns Continuously** - Builds knowledge from every interaction
4. **Networks Proactively** - Connects with partners without manual intervention
5. **Reports Transparently** - Keeps owners informed of all actions
